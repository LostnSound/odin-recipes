Lasagna

Extra cheesy lasagna with a bit of Mexican flair. If you're looking to spice up your lasagna this is just the recipe.

Combining Chicken, cheese, salsa and sour cream. Mixed and baked to perfection. Layers upon layers of meat and gooey cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneles skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4 inch strips
  • ½ cup of lime juice
  • 2(1 ounce) packets of taco seasoning mix
  • 1½ cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste
