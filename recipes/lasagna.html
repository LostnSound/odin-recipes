Lasagna

Extra cheesy lasagna with a bit of Mexican flair. If you're looking to spice up your lasagna this is just the recipe.

Combine chicken, lime juice and taco seasoning in a bowl. You'll want to cook the chicken first. mixe the cheese, salsa and sour cream. Season with salt and pepper.Layer the ingredients between noodles and cover with cheese. Bake in preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour
